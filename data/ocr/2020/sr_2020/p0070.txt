 Efficient and Effective Environmental Protection Activities 
In fiscal 2019, the NTT DATA Group’s environmental costs consisted of ¥5 million in investments and ¥464 million in expenses. 
Compared with fiscal 2018, there was an increase in resource recycling costs (expenses), included in business area costs, in 
fiscal 2019 due to measures to control asbestos, reduce office waste and conserve office paper.
