Environment 
■⑧ Respond to climate change
Formulate a long-term plan to curb greenhouse gas emissions by 
improving the energy efficiency of our data centers and office 
buildings. 
Community 
■⑨ Promote IT education
Deploy our core business strengths to provide IT literacy and 
skills to children, the general public, NPOs/NGOs and so forth. 
Disaster risk 
■⑩ Respond to disaster risks
Develop a business continuity plan (BCP) and continuously 
improve it to minimize potential damage.

■⑪ Ensure compliance
Disseminate and instill our Global Compliance Policy to prevent 
violations of laws and regulations.

■⑫ Promote a responsible supply chain

Guidelines for CSR in the Supply Chain and the like.
