World Cleanup Day
Global Giving Campaign
Regions across 27 countries participated in the World Cleanup Day cleanup activity conducted in
September 2025. More than 1,800 employees collected over 16,400 kg of waste.
Regions across 30 countries participated in the Global Giving Campaign donation activity conducted by
employees in December 2024. More than 2,800 employees collected a total of over $66,600, along with a
large amount of food and many other items. A similar global event is planned for fiscal 2025.
