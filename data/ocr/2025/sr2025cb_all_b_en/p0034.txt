Key Reference Data and Calculation Methods
④ Number of passbook-free bank accounts ｜889,452 accounts
Number of shinkin bank accounts that no longer require a paper passbook (Results as of August 2025)
・ Making accessing financial services through apps more convenient for regional businesses and individuals makes
passbooks less necessary. For financial institutions, passbook-free bank accounts also reduce costs related to printing,
posting and storing passbooks and lowen the ongoing burden of maintaining passbook printing machines. NTT DATA
will continue to provide value through digital technology with the aim of further expanding the number of passbookfree bank accounts.
③ Number of app-based bank accounts ｜ 1,580,000 accounts
Number of bank accounts registered for passbook-free apps or app-based banking (Results as of August 2025)
・ Results point to a certain level of widespread adoption among generations with high digital affinity. However, overall
user numbers suggest that this indicator can be improved further, and NTT DATA will work to increase the number of
accounts using apps by expanding security measures, enhancing links with other services, and improving convenience
for app users.
