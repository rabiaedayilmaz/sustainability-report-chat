 The NTT Group’s Long-Term Vision: “NTT Green Innovation toward 2040” 
In September 2021, the NTT Group formulated its new environment and energy vision, “NTT Green Innovation toward 2040.” 
The vision aims to simultaneously achieve “zero environmental impact” and “economic growth” by “Reduction of Environmental 
Impact through Business Activities” and “Creation of Breakthrough Innovation” to realize a well-being society. Based on this 
vision, the NTT Group, including the NTT DATA Group, aims to achieve carbon neutrality by fiscal 2040.
