 Risk Management Category 
 
 
 Risk Management Training 
The NTT DATA Group conducts ongoing training regarding the legal and other roles of newly appointed directors (including those 
who are also Audit and Supervisory Committee members) and Audit and Supervisory Committee members at NTT DATA and 
Group companies to ensure rigorous risk management throughout the Group. 
In fiscal 2021, we provided training to newly appointed NTT DATA directors at the time of their appointment concerning their 
duties and responsibilities regarding corporate governance and under the Companies Act. In September 2021, we conducted 
training for executives of Group companies in Japan. We will promote increased risk management awareness and enhanced 
response capabilities by implementing various types of training on an ongoing basis.
