Payment of Subcontract Proceeds, Etc. to

*2 CECTRUST, Electronic

This ASP service enables 
electronic exchange 
of contracts between 
companies. It was 
jointly developed with 
Construction-ec.com Co., 
Ltd., based on NTT DATA’s 
SecurePod (a Digital 
Document Exchange

electronic legal documents 
under the April 2001 IT 
Comprehensive Law.

through long-term storage 
and third-party verification 
of original documents.

Procurement Policy
Our purchasing transactions 
are based on three policies.
1. The Company shall carry 
out open and transparent 
purchasing with due 
consideration to business 
needs.
2. We shall provide 
opportunities for 
competition to suppliers, 
regardless of whether 
they are from Japan or 
overseas.
3. The Company shall 
procure competitive, 
appealing products that 
are attuned to business 
needs on a global basis 
and in accordance with 
market principles.
