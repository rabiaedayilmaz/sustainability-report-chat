NTT DATA provides information systems that support 
society as a whole, including taxation, 
employment/labor, trade, and transportation systems, as 
well as systems and services that make communities 
safe, convenient, and prosperous. In these ways, we help 
create social infrastructure with an end-user perspective.

Business Fields

and security of people’s health and lives, including 
healthcare management systems, a regional health 
information network system, and a wide-area disaster 
and emergency medical information system.
Medical and Healthcare Sector
We deliver systems and services that raise the efficiency 
and improve the quality of services of a wide range of 
financial institutions. These include governmental 
financial organizations, securities firms, insurance 
companies, banks, credit banks, credit unions, labor 
banks, and agricultural cooperatives. 
Financial Sector
We contribute to the realization of a cashless society 
through the building of electronic money centers and the 
development and operation of network systems that link 
payments and receipts collected by financial institutions, 
retailers, credit companies, and government agencies.
