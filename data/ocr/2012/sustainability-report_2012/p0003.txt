Organizations covered
The report centers on the activities of 
the NTT DATA Group in Japan. Some 
sections in the report also cover the 
activities of the worldwide 
NTT DATA Group and Group companies 
outside Japan.
Reported activities
The report covers initiatives and results 
for the economic, social, and 
environmental protection activities of 
the NTT DATA Corporation and 
Group companies.
Period of coverage
The report centers on initiatives 
undertaken in fiscal 2012 (April 1, 2011 
to March 31, 2012). Some coverage is 
given to previous periods, fiscal 2013 
activities, and activities planned for 
the future.
Note: With respect to the notation of fiscal years 
in the graphs and charts within this CSR 
Report, “2012” indicates the fiscal year 
ended March 31, 2012.
