Environment
The NTT DATA Group is contributing to the greening of its clients and society through 
IT while making a Groupwide effort to reduce the environmental impact of its own 
business activities.
NTT DATA Sustainability Report 2018
In addressing global environmental issues, it is important to ensure sustainability from a wider 
perspective that includes responding to climate change and conserving biodiversity. The NTT DATA 
Group is working to resolve a range of environmental issues. In addition to the “Greening of Our 
Group,” based on an environmentally oriented management approach that gives due consideration to 
the natural environment in every aspect of its corporate activities, the Group is continuing to promote 
progressive initiatives in the greening of clients and society as a whole through its products and 
services. In order to achieve significant results in improving environmental issues, the Company is 
also aiming to enhance its environmental management while promoting Groupwide “environmental 
contribution and communication.”
