Overseas Sales Expansion
Since around 2005, NTT DATA has been engaged in fullfledged global expansion. In fiscal 2017, overseas sales 
grew to over ¥900.0 billion among consolidated net sales 
of approximately ¥2.1 trillion.
 
Going forward, we believe the expansion of overseas 
sales will be a major driver of corporate growth.
 
We aim to expand overseas sales by organic growth 
leveraging global synergies as well as M&As and increase 
our local presence.
