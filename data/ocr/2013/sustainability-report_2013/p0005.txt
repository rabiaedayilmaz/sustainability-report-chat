CSR in the NTT DATA Group Sustainable Expansion

Business Fields Innovative, Global

Dialog with Stakeholders Engagement
 Organizations covered
The report centers on the activities of the NTT DATA 
Group in Japan. Some sections in the report also cover 
the activities of the worldwide NTT DATA Group and 
Group companies outside Japan.
 Reported activities
The report covers initiatives and results for the economic, social, and environmental protection activities 
of the NTT DATA Corporation and Group companies.
 Period of coverage
The report centers on initiatives undertaken in 
fiscal 2013 (April 1, 2012 to March 31, 2013). 
Some coverage is given to previous periods, fiscal 2014 
activities, and activities planned for the future.
Note: With respect to the notation of fiscal years 
in the graphs and charts within this CSR Report, 
“2013” indicates the fiscal year ended March 31, 2013.
 Guidelines referenced in this report
 
• Ministry of the Environment 
Environmental Reporting Guidelines (2012)
 
• Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) 
Sustainability Reporting Guidelines, Version 3.1 
(G3.1)
