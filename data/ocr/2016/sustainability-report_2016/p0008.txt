In order for NTT DATA to establish a competitive foundation in the global IT services industry, we must ensure a definite 
presence in the markets of each country. To this end, the Global 2nd Stage aims to achieve consolidated net sales of ¥2 trillion 
and an overseas sales ratio of 50% by around 2020. 
Under the new Medium-Term Management Strategy (from the fiscal years ending March 31, 2017 to 2019), we will move 
toward the establishment of a global brand in the Global 2nd Stage with increasing local presence in each country and region 
as the core strategic theme.
