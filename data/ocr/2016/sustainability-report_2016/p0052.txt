 Improving Activity Levels through Periodic Internal Audits
Internal Environmental Audits
The NTT DATA Group periodically conducts internal environmental audits to confirm conformance with ISO 14001 
specifications and PDCA cycle functionality.
In fiscal 2016, we conducted two actual audits carried out by internal environmental auditors from organizations other 
than the one being audited. In addition, internal environmental auditors conducted two environmental audits of their own 
organizations on a self-audit basis.
We hold meetings before and after audits to confirm important audit items and the status of Group-wide environmental 
management systems, as well as to share suggestions, improvements and other information as part of a drive to bolster the 
levels of internal environmental auditing and Group environmental protection activities.
