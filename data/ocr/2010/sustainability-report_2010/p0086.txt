Contributing to office paper usage reduction with an authenticated print solution 
In May 2009, NTT DATA began offering the inexpensive u:ma Authenticated Print solution for multiple 
vendors. Using the u:ma-G card reader, which can be used by a variety of smart cards, the user is 
authorized to easily print only the necessary material. 
This helps prevent printing errors and neglecting documents of uncertain ownership, and reduces 
the risks of print job intermixing. This helps to reduce paper use by 20% (based on test figures provided 
by existing NTT DATA customers and other publicly announced results).
