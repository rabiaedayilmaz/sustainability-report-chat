Valentine’s Day 
In step with the objectives of the NTT DATA Valentine’s Day 
campaign, the two companies jointly conduct as part of their 
social contribution activities NTT DATA CHUGOKU Valentine’s 
Day synchronized to February 14. Cakes, cookies and other 
sundries produced at welfare workplaces in Minami-ku, 
Hiroshima, which is located in the NTT DATA Hijiyama Building, 
are sold in-house at prices that include a donation to support 
the independence of people with disabilities.

M.I.S.I. Co., Ltd. 
Participation in Lee 
National Denim Day 
In concordance with the aims of Lee National Denim Day, 
M.I.S.I. participates in the day every year, and equally matches 
donations from employees. Donations are used in support of 
research and educational programs toward the eradication of 
breast cancer. During fiscal 2010, M.I.S.I. donated a total of 
$588.
