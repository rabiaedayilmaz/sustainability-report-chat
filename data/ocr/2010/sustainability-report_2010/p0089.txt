【Additional Topics】 
 
NTT DATA FRONTIER CORPORATION 
 Joined a joint campaign of clean-up activities at five locations in Minato-ku, 
 
Tokyo, in June and September 2009 (27 participants). 
 
 
 
 
 
NTT DATA SOFIA CORPORATION 
Took part in the Tokyo Bay Clean-Up Campaign (waterfront cleaning) held by 
Minato Sports, Community, Culture and Health Foundation in September and 
November 2009 (26 participants). 
 
 
 
 
NTT DATA SYSTEM TECHNOLOGIES INC. 
Took part in the December 2009 biannual Clean Walk (cleaning of flower 
beds) as a Flower Supporter of NPO Hana Kaido (two participants).

NTT DATA TOKAI CORPORATION 
 Carried out monthly clean-up activities in the vicinities of the Fushimi and 
Aoi buildings (total of 392 participants). 
 
 
 
 
 
【Chugoku Region】 
NTT DATA CHUGOKU CORPORATION 
Joined in the Ota River Mass Clean-up, organized by City of Hiroshima Clean Ota River Executive 
Committee, in July 2009, involving a riverside trash-gathering walk (nine participants).

 Took part in the Peace Memorial Park Mass Clean-Up, organized by the 
City of Hiroshima in July 2009, in preparation for the Peace Ceremony (25 
participants).
