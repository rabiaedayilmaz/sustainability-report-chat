 Future of Work  
 P68
New ways of working that enhance employee experience and performance. 
Provide and promote work style reforms for society as a whole.
Amid the global shortage of highly skilled IT personnel, it is necessary to secure and 
maintain excellent human resources by promoting flexible working styles that meet 
the diverse needs of employees, implementing appropriate evaluations, and fostering 
an attractive corporate culture. To this end, it is essential to support employees’ career 
development and flexible working styles. The Group will provide new ways of working 
to improve performance and EX (Employee Experience), while at the same time 
promoting work-style reform for society as a whole.
