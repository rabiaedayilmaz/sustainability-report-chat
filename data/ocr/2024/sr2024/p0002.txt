Editorial Policy
The NTT DATA Sustainability Report 2024 provides comprehensive and detailed non-financial information to all stakeholders with 
a focus on NTT DATA’s approach to non-financial matters and specific initiatives taken in fiscal 2023 aimed at promoting sustainability management. From this fiscal year, we have enhanced our response to the disclosure requirements of the SASB Standards.
