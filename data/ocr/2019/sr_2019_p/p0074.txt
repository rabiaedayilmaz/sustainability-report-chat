The rising concentration of greenhouse gases and other factors are impacting the climate in ways 
that have led to the increased severity and frequency of natural disasters such as typhoons and 
floods while also significantly impacting everyday life across society and industry. In December 
2015, the Paris Agreement, which serves as a new international framework for global warming 
countermeasures beyond 2020, was adopted at the 2015 United Nations Climate Change 
Conference (COP21). Addressing climate change was also included in the United Nations SDGs 
adopted in September 2015. As illustrated by these developments, response to climate change 
in terms of mitigation and adaptation has gained even greater significance as a common global 
concern. The NTT DATA Group is pursuing strategic initiatives through its business with the 
intention of offering a more effective response to climate change.
