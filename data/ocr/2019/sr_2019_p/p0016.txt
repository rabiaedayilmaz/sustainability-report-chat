In July 2012, the NTT DATA Group set up its first advisory board comprising business owners 
and academic experts.
During the past three terms over six years, a total of 17 outside experts advised us from 
experts’ points of view on ways to address management issues of the Company and initiatives 
to expand global business, which we incorporated into our business administration. In February 
2019, the fourth advisory board was set up.
We will incorporate their opinions and proposals, which are based on the members’ exchange 
of views on aspects of our management and on societal issues, into business activities.
