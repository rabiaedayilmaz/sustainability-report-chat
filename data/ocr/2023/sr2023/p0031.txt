NTT DATA Sustainability Report 2023 Data Book 
Information Disclosure Based on the TCFD 
 
In March 2021, NTT DATA expressed its support for the recommendations of the Task Force on Climate-related Financial 
Disclosures (TCFD). Since then, we have been promoting climate change initiatives across the entire organization and ensuring 
transparency in our external activities. We consider addressing climate change as a critical management issue. We conduct 
analysis and assessments following the TCFD framework regarding climate change risks and opportunities. We also actively 
explore strategies for addressing long-term climate change risks and opportunities and are working to strengthen information 
disclosure. In fiscal 2022, NTT DATA’s disclosure of sustainability information in the fiscal 2022 Securities Report was selected 
as a good practice by Japan’s Financial Services Agency*. 
*Our efforts are showcased as a good practice on the Agency’s website: https://www.fsa.go.jp/news/r4/singi/20230131/00.html (Japanese only)
