・The deputy director in charge of technology management oversees the division of responsibilities covering 
technological R&D (DCTO), quality assurance, and security (DCISO). 
Personal information 
protection managers 
(responsible persons) 
These officers with responsibilities and authority for planning and operating the personal

Group-wide perspective.
