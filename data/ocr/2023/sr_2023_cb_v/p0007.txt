NTT DATA Business Solutions (Germany)
Companies are increasingly expected to expand the
release of sustainability-related products while ensuring
proper
compliance
with
numerous
related
laws,
highlighting the need for effective data management.
Despite this, many companies still rely on manual data
management,
posing
challenges
in
streamlining
operations and preventing human error.
NTT DATA Business Solutions has initiated a joint
project with clients to address this challenge. We have
developed a centralized database model capable of
storing
approximately
1,000
materials,
with

attributes each, equipped only with essential functions.
This facilitates targeted traceability and collaboration
with relevant stakeholders through the database.
Integration with the SAP Material Master enables
various
simulations,
including
carbon
footprint
calculations, chemical component management, and
more,
at
the product
level,
component
level,
and
component structure level.
Efforts will be made to expand functionality going
forward, such as calculating the CO2 emissions of
products, thereby contributing to the development and
market
distribution
of
sustainable
products
for
companies.
