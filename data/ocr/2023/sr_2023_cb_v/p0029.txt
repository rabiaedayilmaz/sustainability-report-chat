NTT DATA has developed a platform called fowald
to
break
down
barriers
between
corporations,
municipalities,
and
residents,
fostering
collaboration
toward the common goal of achieving a sustainable
society. On this platform, corporations and municipalities
can publish themed “quests”, which residents can easily
engage with. Those who resonate with these quests can
engage in community contribution activities in their local
or affiliated area, post about their experiences, and
contribute
back
to
supported
causes,
affiliated
companies, or communities. Furthermore, corporations
and
municipalities
can
digitally
visualize
residents’
community
contribution
activities,
allowing
for
quantitative and qualitative measurement to drive further
improvements, enhance external appeal, and improve
brand image.
In past experiments, we collaborated with shopping
district promotion associations on photo contests to
revitalize
the
community,
garnering
enthusiastic
participation and making significant contributions to local
revitalization. This approach also led to increased
participant
engagement
when
applied
to
internal
contribution activities.
Through fowald
, NTT DATA not only supports
individuals in taking the first step toward sustainable
activities but also contributes to creating an environment
conducive to their ongoing implementation.
