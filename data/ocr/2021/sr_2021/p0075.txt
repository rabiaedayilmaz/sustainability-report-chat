Sales from our data centers and related 
services amounted to ¥58.7 billion in fiscal 
2020. Under our business plan, we expect

We build and operate data centers with high 
environmental performance, including the Mitaka Data 
Center, and plan to promote further renewable energy 
use, energy conservation and new energy management

We estimate the financial impact of providing 
flexible, location-neutral work styles to be 
¥58,980 million, based on our superior

CO2 emissions. 
The cost of providing teleworking arrangements, 
including participating in Telework Days and using our
