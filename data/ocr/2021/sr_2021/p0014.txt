12 Material ESG Issues and KPIs 
 
In fiscal 2020, we were able to achieve most of the targets (KPIs) set by each organization for the 12 material ESG issues. 
Thanks to the efforts of each organization to achieve the KPIs, moreover, we disseminated our social contribution policy to each 
and every employee. In fiscal 2021, we will continue to further accelerate ESG management by working to achieve the KPIs 
established by each organization based on our policies of “social contribution through business” and “social contribution through 
corporate activities.”
