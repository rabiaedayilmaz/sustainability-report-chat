Respond to Climate Change 
 
The rising concentration of greenhouse gases and other factors are impacting the climate in various ways, resulting in the 
increased severity and frequency of natural disasters, such as typhoons and floods, while significantly affecting everyday life 
across society and industry. In December 2015, the Paris Agreement, a new international framework for global warming 
countermeasures beyond 2020, was adopted at the 2015 UN Climate Change Conference (COP21) and came into effect in 
November 2016. Addressing climate change was also included as one of the SDGs adopted by the UN General Assembly in 
September 2015. As illustrated by these developments, responding to climate change in terms of mitigation and adaptation has 
gained even greater significance as a common global concern. The NTT DATA Group is pursuing strategic initiatives through its 
business with the intention of more effectively addressing climate change.
