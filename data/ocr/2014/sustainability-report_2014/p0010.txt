 CSR in the NTT DATA Group
CSR Message
We shall continue our innovations and contributions utilizing IT
to realize a prosperous, sustainable society.
The NTT DATA Group, as a corporation aiding innovation through IT, will create improved paradigms 
and contribute to solving human, social and environmental problems.
 The NTT Group CSR Charter
The NTT Group CSR Priority Activities were defined to 
further consolidate the NTT Group in its drive to address 
social issues in Japan and overseas. This step aids the NTT 
Group in promoting activities on an autonomous and 
voluntary basis.
