An Overview of the NTT DATA Group’s Environmental Impact 
The environmental impact of the NTT DATA Group’s business activities derives chiefly from the emission of greenhouse gases 
through the consumption of energy centering on electricity. Lloyd’s Register Quality Assurance Limited has been charged with the 
responsibility of providing a third-party verification of the Group’s input of energy and greenhouse gas emissions from fiscal 2014.
