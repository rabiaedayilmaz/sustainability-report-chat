NTT DATA 
Getronics 
Corporation 
Publishing SPAZIO, a 
Cultural Magazine 
Carrying on certain elements of the corporate 
culture of Olivetti Corporation of Japan, NTT 
DATA Getronics’ forerunner, the company 
publishes SPAZIO, a cultural magazine that 
seeks to cultivate overall ties between 
European and Japanese culture. In 
publication since 1970—for more than 40 
years—the magazine highlights world cultural 
and artistic themes, centering on Europe.
