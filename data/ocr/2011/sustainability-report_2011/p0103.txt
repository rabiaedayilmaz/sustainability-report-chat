In-house and external dissemination of Green IT information 
We are sharing the latest Green IT-related trends and quantitative assessments on 
environmental impact reductions throughout the NTT DATA Group. This helps with 
environmentally responsible system development and sales activities. Furthermore, we 
disseminate Green IT information to customers and society at large through public websites 
and other channels. 
 
We also provided a broad-based introduction of our environment- and 
energy-related businesses as ITpro EXPO 2010, which was held October 18–20, 2010. 
 
 
 
 
Keane, Inc. (United States) 
Initiatives to virtualize servers and consolidate data centers 
As reducing greenhouse gas emissions requires IT equipment to consume less power, 
Keane, Inc., is mounting proactive initiatives to virtualize its servers in India and consolidate 
its data centers in India and the United States. Since 2009, the company has consolidated 
three data centers and two server rooms in its efforts toward greener IT.
