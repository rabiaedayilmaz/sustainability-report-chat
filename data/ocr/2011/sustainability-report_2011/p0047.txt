1/67 
NTT DATA CSR Report 2011 
 
 
Trust by customers, shareholders, suppliers, the society and others is indispensable for a 
company to continuously grow. A company can be trusted upon by recognizing own social 
responsibility, meeting expectations of customers and other stakeholders through 
communications with them and enhancing their satisfaction. 
The NTT DATA Group is a company group that is socially committed to create new 
“mechanisms” and “values” using the information technology and to realize a more affluent 
and harmonious society. For global environmental matters especially, we as NTT DATA 
Group will undertake activities to reduce the environmental load by utilizing the Information 
technology and contribute to resolving issues that the Earth and our society are facing. It is 
in this recognition that we at the NTT DATA Group believe that it is the principle for 
companies to gain trust by our society through the compliance with laws, regulations and 
contracts within and beyond the confines of the country, respect international norms 
including human rights, and pursue actions, based on a social good sense and fair and 
transparent business activities. 
Based on the foregoing belief, the NTT DATA Group will do its utmost to act in accordance 
with the “Basic Stance on Business Ethics” to be a trustworthy company group.
