Acquiring ISMS, Privacy Mark and other third-party accreditations 
Within the NTT DATA Group, necessary units that handle confidential and personal 
information hold ISO 27001 information security management system certification. As of 
March 31, 2011, the relevant departments of 38 NTT DATA Group companies, including NTT 
DATA, had acquired this certification. 
In addition, NTT DATA and 29 other Group companies were authorized by the Japan 
Institute for Promotion of Digital Economy and Community to use the Privacy Mark. 
 
Eight individual services have acquired ITSMS* certification. 
 
*ITSMS (IT Service Management System): An efficient, effective management system for IT service 
providers. ITSMS certification means third-party certification of a service management system.
