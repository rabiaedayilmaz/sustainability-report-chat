More than 53% of retired workers opt for reemployment 
In February 1999, the NTT DATA Group established a Career Staff System that caters for 
employees who have reached the mandatory retirement age, but wish to continue working 
until 65. 
 
This system offers an array of work-style options that are flexible to participants’ 
requirements, including full-time employment, reduced working hours, and three- and 
four-day working weeks. Moreover, during fiscal 2009 we supplemented the existing
