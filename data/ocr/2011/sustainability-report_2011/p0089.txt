Promoting efforts to acquire the NTT Group’s Solution Environmental 
Label 
Since October 2009, the NTT Group has certified environmentally friendly solutions and 
assigned Solution Environmental Labels* to ICT solutions with an environmental impact 
reduction effect above a specified level. 
During fiscal 2011, NTT DATA acquired three Solution Environmental Labels, increasing its 
total to seven. We plan to increase our number of environmental labels. 
 
* Solution Environmental Label: A certification provided environmentally friendly solutions that employ ICT 
solutions to quantitatively assess their CO2 reduction effect and achieve a reduction rate of more than 15%.
